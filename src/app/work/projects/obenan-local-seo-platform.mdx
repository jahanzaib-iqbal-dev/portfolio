## Overview

Obenan (obenan.ai) is a local SEO and digital presence management platform that helps businesses manage their online listings, reviews, and local search visibility. I joined as the first technical hire and built the product from the ground up — from initial architecture decisions to scaling the platform to serve thousands of businesses and reach $1M+ in annual recurring revenue.

## The Challenge

The founders had a clear vision for a local SEO product but no technical team. They needed someone who could own the entire technical stack, make architecture decisions, build the product, and eventually grow and lead the engineering team — all while shipping features fast enough to compete in a crowded market.

## My Role

As Technical Team Lead, I was responsible for:

- **Architecture & Technical Decisions**: Designed the entire system architecture from day one — database schema, API design, service boundaries, and deployment infrastructure.
- **Full-Stack Development**: Built both the frontend dashboard and backend APIs using JavaScript/TypeScript, React, Node.js, and PostgreSQL.
- **Team Building**: Grew the engineering team from just myself to a full squad, hiring engineers, establishing code review practices, and creating development workflows.
- **Product Engineering**: Worked directly with the founders to translate business requirements into technical specifications and shipped features on aggressive timelines.
- **Cross-Border Collaboration**: Managed a hybrid team distributed across Pakistan and the Netherlands, establishing async communication patterns and delivery cadence.

## Technical Stack

- **Frontend**: React.js, Next.js, TypeScript
- **Backend**: Node.js, Express, NestJS
- **Database**: PostgreSQL, Redis
- **Infrastructure**: AWS, Docker, CI/CD with GitHub Actions
- **Integrations**: Google Business Profile API, Google Maps API, Google Places API, Review management APIs

## Key Achievements

- **Zero to $1M+ ARR**: Built the product from the first line of code to a revenue-generating platform with thousands of active business users.
- **Platform Scale**: Engineered the system to handle tens of thousands of business listings, automated review responses, and real-time local SEO analytics.
- **API Integrations**: Built deep integrations with Google Business Profile, Google Maps, and multiple review platforms — the core value proposition of the product.
- **Engineering Culture**: Established TDD practices, code review standards, automated testing pipelines, and continuous delivery workflows that enabled the team to ship confidently and frequently.
- **4+ Year Partnership**: Maintained a long-term engagement from January 2022 to March 2026, growing with the product through every stage from MVP to scale.

## Lessons Learned

Building Obenan taught me that the hardest part of taking a product from zero to $1M+ ARR isn't the code — it's making the right architectural decisions early enough that you don't have to rewrite everything when growth hits. Investing in solid foundations (proper database design, clean API boundaries, automated testing) pays dividends when you need to move fast at scale.

This experience directly shaped how I run Global Software Consulting today: we build MVPs that are production-grade from day one, because we've seen firsthand what happens when you cut corners early.
